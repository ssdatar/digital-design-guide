Fonts

Publico is the predominant typeface used in our newspapers and is at the core of our typographic identity. It has the widest range of usage – from body text to headlines to the AJC namemplate. It's a workhorse that is appropriate for both news and features. It is not meant to be used all caps.

Scout is a fresh, modern sans serif introduced to provide contrast in a Publico world. It's used as the lede headline on news display pages in print, for labels and briefs headlines. It works great all caps, and online, it works great in thin with lots of letter spacing.
